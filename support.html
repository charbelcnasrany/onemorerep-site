Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? Open RepCall, go to the You tab, then Settings, then Delete account. This permanently removes your data.
  • My check-in shows "unverified." Check-ins verify when you're at a recognised workout venue. If yours isn't recognised, you can add it from the check-in screen, or connect WHOOP or your watch so a real workout earns the verified badge.
  • Report or block someone. Use the menu on any post to report it, or block a user from their profile. We review reports and act within 24 hours.
