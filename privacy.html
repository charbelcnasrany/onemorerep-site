OneMoreRep — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 7, 2026 · OneMoreRep is currently in private development/testing.

What OneMoreRep is

OneMoreRep is a social fitness app: you check in at a gym, and friends you have explicitly added can see your check-ins and cheer you on.

What we collect

  • Account: your email address and password (managed by Supabase Auth).
  • Profile: the display name, username, and profile photo you choose.
  • Check-ins: when you check in, we record the time and your location at that moment to verify you are at a gym (using Google Places). We do NOT track your location continuously — only at the instant you check in.
  • Photos/videos: only what you capture in the app at check-in. Stored privately; visible only to your accepted friends.
  • WHOOP data (optional): if you choose to connect WHOOP, we read your workout start/end times to confirm a workout happened around your check-in. We do not read or store health metrics beyond this. You can disconnect at any time in the app, which deletes our access immediately.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not show your data to anyone except friends you have added.
  • We do not track your location in the background.
  • We do not run advertising or share data with advertisers.

Where data lives

Data is stored with Supabase (database and file storage) with row-level security: only you and your accepted friends can access your content. Wearable access tokens are stored server-side only and never on your device or exposed to other users.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual check-ins in the app. To delete your account and all associated data, email us and we will remove it promptly.

Children

OneMoreRep is not directed at children under 16.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update this page and note the new effective date.
