Delete your account

You can permanently delete your RepCall account and everything associated with it at any time. Here's how.

Delete it inside the app (instant)

  1. Open RepCall and go to the You tab.
  2. Open Settings (the gear icon).
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and its data are removed straight away.

Can't get into the app?

Email hello@repcall.app from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We'll verify the request and process it within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your profile (name, username, avatar, home gym)
  • All of your check-ins, including any photos or videos and Progress Vault items
  • Your streaks, reactions, comments, nudges, and other social activity
  • Your friend connections
  • Any connected wearable (e.g. WHOOP) authorization and the workout stats pulled from it
  • Your push-notification token

This is permanent and cannot be undone. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days. We may retain the minimum records required by law where applicable.
